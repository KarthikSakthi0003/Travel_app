About the hotel

Enjoy recreational amenities such as an indoor pool and a fitness center. Additional amenities at this hotel include complimentary wireless internet access and a fireplace in the lobby.You can enjoy a meal at Kijus Restaurant serving the guests of Hampton Inn by Hilton Sydney, or stop in at the snack bar/deli. A complimentary buffet breakfast is served daily from 6 AM to 10 AM.Featured amenities include complimentary wired internet access, a 24-hour business center, and express check-out. Planning an event in Sydney? This hotel has 1500 square feet (139 square meters) of space consisting of a conference center and meeting rooms. Free self parking is available onsite.Make yourself at home in one of the 128 guestrooms featuring refrigerators and LCD televisions. Your pillowtop bed comes with premium bedding. Wired and wireless internet access is complimentary, while MP3 docking stations and digital programming provide entertainment.
